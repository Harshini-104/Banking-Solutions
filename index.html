FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a financial instrument that allows you to invest a lump sum for a fixed tenure at a guaranteed rate of return.

Is my investment safe?

Yes, your investment is secure and protected. Fixed Deposits offer guaranteed returns with minimal risk.

Can I withdraw my FD before maturity?

Premature withdrawal is allowed, subject to applicable terms and a small penalty on interest.

Do Fixed Deposits offer loan facilities?

Yes, you can avail of a loan against your Fixed Deposit without breaking it.
